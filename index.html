Biography

I am a third-year PhD student in University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (2020-). Before that, I got my Bachelor degree in Dept. of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University (2016-2020).

My research interests include deep learning, computer vision and AI security. Recently I am working on Face Forgery Detection.
