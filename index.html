Biography

Chandler Timm C. Doloriel is a researcher at the Singapore University of Technology & Design, where he is currently engaged in the study of AI methods for detecting artificially generated content. Holding an MSc in Electrical Engineering, he brings a deep understanding of machine learning algorithms, particularly in the realms of computer vision and deep learning.

Outside of his professional and academic pursuits, Chandler is passionate about physical fitness and frequently visits the gym to maintain a healthy lifestyle. His culinary tastes are an eclectic mix, with favorites ranging from chicken biryani and chicken curry to Filipino classics like adobo, sinigang, and mongo soup. A lover of nature, Chandler enjoys peaceful walks in the forest and avoids the hustle and bustle of big cities, preferring quieter, more serene environments.

As he prepares for potential doctoral studies, his research interests include fundamental machine learning and the integration of AI into vision systems for specialized applications. He is committed to advancing the field by tackling complex problems in AI and machine learning.
