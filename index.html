About Me

I am a PhD researcher focusing on test‑time adaptation and its applications to aquaculture monitoring, advised by Habib Ullah, Kristian Hovde Liland, Fadi Al Machot, Muhammad Salman Siddiqui, and Tor Kristian Stevik. Previously, I was a Senior Research Assistant at Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD) under Ngai‑Man Cheung on deepfake detection. Outside research, I occasionally practice muay-thai, swimming, and run marathons (half).
