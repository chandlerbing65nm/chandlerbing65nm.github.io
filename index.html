About Me

My research focuses on leveraging AI to: (1) optimize aquaculture feeding practices through audio processing, (2) develop advanced underwater vision systems for precise monitoring and analysis, and (3) deploy lightweight AI models to enhance operational performance and resource management.

My PhD advisers are Dr. Habib Ullah, Dr. Kristian Hovde Liland, Dr. Fadi Al Machot, and Dr. Muhammad Salman Siddiqui.

Prior to my PhD, I was supervised by Dr. Ngai-Man Cheung at SUTD, where I focused on visual deepfake detection.

Outside of my research, my hobbies include martial arts, swimming, and participating in half-marathons.
