About Me

I am currently researching AI methods to improve the quality of life for aquaculture species. My research includes feeding intensity classification, fish behavior analysis, fish counting and detection, and other related topics that can be enhanced through AI and machine learning.

Outside of my research duties, I am passionate about sports and health. I regularly run distances from 5km to 21km and cycle at least once a week.
